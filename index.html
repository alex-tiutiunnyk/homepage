BREED OVERVIEW

Group:Non-Sporting

Height:14.5 to 16.5 inches (male); 13.5 to 15.5 inches (female)

Weight: Around 23 pounds (male), Around 17 pounds (female)

Coat: Stiff and straight outer coat and a soft and thick undercoat

Coat color: Three color types: red, black and sesame, black and tan

Life expectancy: 13 to 16 years

HISTORY OF THE SHIBA INU

The Shiba Inu is an ancient Japanese breed that was originally used for flushing out quarry for their hunter owners.

Shiba Inus are currently the most popular breed in Japan, and they have a growing fanbase in the United States and other parts of the world.

PROS

  • Clean; no high-maintenance grooming regime
  • Not prone to barking
  • Not a clingy, 'velcro' dog

CONS

  • Can be aloof and independent
  • They don't always get on with other dogs
  • High prey drive, and a reliable recall can be elusive
